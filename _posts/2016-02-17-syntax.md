## Operators

The Relapse langauge consists of several operators:

|-----------+----------------------------+---------------------------|
| symbol    | name                       | example                   |
|:---------:|:--------------------------:|:-------------------------:|
|`->`       | LeafNode                   | `->eq($int, 1)`           |
|[shorthands](http://127.0.0.1:4000/doc/builtin.html#Shorthands) | LeafNode                   | `== 1`                      |
|`*`        | ZAny (Zero or more of anything) |  *                   |
|`:`        | TreeNode                   | `A:*`                       |
|`<empty>`  | Empty                      | `A:<empty>`                 |
|`[,]`      | Concat                     | `[A:*, *]`                  |
|`(|)`      | Or                         | `(A:* | B:*)`               |
|`(&)`      | And                        | `(A:* & _ == 1)`            |
|`()*`      | ZeroOrMore                 | `(A == 1)*`                 |
|`# @ =`    | Reference                  | `#main=@a #a=*`             |
|`!()`      | Not                        | `!(A == 1)`                 |
|`.`        | Contains                   | `.A == 1`                   |
|`()?`      | Optional                   | `(A == 1)?`                 |
|`{;}`      | Interleave                 | `{A:*;B:*}`                 |
|-----------+----------------------------+---------------------------|
