## Shorthands

|-----------+----------------------------+---------------------------|
| shorthand | function                   | example                   |
|:---------:|:--------------------------:|:-------------------------:|
|::         | type                       | :: $bool                  |
|==         | eq (equal)                 | == 1                      |
|!=         | ne (not equal)             | != 1                      |
|<          | lt (less than)             | < 1                       |
|>          | gt (greater than)          | > 1                       |
|<=         | le (less than or equal)    | <= 1                      |
|>=         | ge (greater than or equal) | >= 1                      |
|*=         | contains                   | *= "substring"            |
|^=         | hasPrefix                  | ^= "abc"                  |
|$=         | hasSuffix                  | $= "xyz"                  |
|~=         | regex                      | ~= "^abc.*xyz$"           |
|-----------+----------------------------+---------------------------|
